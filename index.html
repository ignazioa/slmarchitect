Laura is a highly motivated architect that strives to find the right balance between customer's demands and contingents requirements without compromising sustainability.

After her MSc in Building Engineering and Architecture from the University of Catania (IT) she moved in The Hague, where she is living and working since 2015.

She is familiar with the Dutch building code through all construction phases (SO/VO/DO/BA/W) however her portfolio includes projects from all over Europe (and some overseas as well!). Among others:
- construction drawings for an industrial warehouse (USA)
- sketch and concept design for a shopping mall (RU)
- Design and visualization of many house’s renovations (EU, mostly in The Netherlands, Italy and France)

Her goal is to support private clients as well as, small and medium size, business in field related to architecture, technical drawings and 3D visualizations.
Her softwares knowledge includes: Revit, Archicad, Sketchup, v-Ray, Enscape, Lumion 3D, and Adobe suite among others.
