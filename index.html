Laura is a skilled architect specialized in housing renovations. Living and working in The Hague since 2015, she has been involved with various perspectives of the construction process, from the first sketches (SO, VO) through building permit(BA) to realization and construction drawings (W) and she is familiar with all main Dutch laws (Bouwbesluit, Woningwet, Bestemmingsplan, Beschermde stadsgezicht, Brandveiligheid enz.). However her portfolio’s includes project located all over the Europe and some overseas as well. among others

  • construction drawings for an industrial warehouse (USA)
  • sketch and concept design for a shopping mall (RU)
  • Apartments and villa’s renovations (all over EU, mostly in The Netherlands, Italy and France)

Actively working towards more sustainable solutions, she’s constantly learning about new techniques or product that can help us reach those Zero energy buildings we have been talking about for at least 2 decades now. DfD and prefab are the way to go to reduce building footprint without compromising its estetics and design flexibility. She currently works as a freelancer to support private clients as well as, small and medium size, business in field related to architecture, technical drawings and 3D visualizations. I am able to work with a wide range of softwares: Revit, Archicad, Sketchup, v-Ray, Cine render, Lumion 3D, Adobe suite.. just to name it a couple. Colleagues know me as a highly motivated draftsman and a creative designer who always strives to find the right balance between customer's demands and contingents requirements. I hold an MSc in Building engineering and Architecture (4/S) from the University of Catania, Italy. Feeling like you could use some help? If so, do not hesitate to get in touch! Always happy to meet new colleagues and hear about their unique perspectives.
