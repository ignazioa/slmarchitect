Laura is a highly motivated architect that strives for sustainability and user's experience.

Familiar with all design phases (from concept to construction) and particularly with the Dutch building code. Even though her portfolio includes projects from all over the world (EU, RU, USA) and spans from warehouses to shopping mall to housing.

She owns a MSc in Building Engineering and Architecture from the University of Catania (IT) and has an extensive softwares knowledge, such as Revit, Archicad, Sketchup, Enscape, Lumion 3D and Adobe Suite. 

Her goal is to support private clients as well as, small and medium size architecture offices.
